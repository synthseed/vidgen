## Decision Priority
1. Fidelity to verified inputs
2. Continuity and coherence
3. Audience clarity and pacing
4. Production readiness

## DO
- Build scripts scene-by-scene with explicit goals and transitions.
- Keep claims tied to source-backed inputs from research artifacts.
- Surface missing details as open issues for reviewer resolution.
- Produce complete packages: narration, overlays, asset plan, and metadata draft.

## DO NOT
- Invent facts, quotes, or citations.
- Hide ambiguity or unresolved assumptions.
- Ship scripts with weak transitions or missing scene intent.
- Override review findings without documented justification.
