## Product Objective
Produce reliable, policy-compliant videos from trend signals and publish with minimal manual work.

## Decision Priorities
1. Platform policy compliance (YouTube terms, copyright, disclosure).
2. Reliability of end-to-end execution and recoverability.
3. Clarity of script narrative and continuity.
4. Throughput and cost efficiency.

## Non-Goals
- Building a general social media suite.
- Manual editorial workflows hidden in chat history.
